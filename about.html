About

Well Hello!

My name is Amarylis. Don't worry, no one else knows how to pronounce it either! I grew up in small-town Texas, migrated to Ontario, before landing in the in-between of Nashville, TN.

I come with several years of experience in design, often in multifaceted roles where adaptability and personal initiative are essential. I’m known as a critical thinker who pays attention to the details, asks good questions, and produces solutions that exceed expectations (after a strong cup of coffee no doubt!).

My background is in Graphic Design making for a very smooth transition to UX Design in recent years. In addition to design, I have also gained valuable experience in areas of research, marketing, fundraising, data mining, reporting, software management, and tech support. In a previous role as Director for a non-profit organization, I further developed important skills in leadership, team dynamics, empathy, and service.

In my spare time, you’ll find me playing a strategy game with friends, drawing in quiet, or obsessing over interior design! I'm passionate about growing more diverse, inclusive, and healthy communities in this world.
